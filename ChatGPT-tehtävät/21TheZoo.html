Information

The Zoo is a free, family-focused zoo which has a small range of exotic and domestic animals and is recognised as one of the most progressivee zoos in the world. There is lots to see and do all year, including events, eye-to-eye encounters, behind the scenes tours and more!

Zoo experiences motivate children to care for the enviroment and this is especially important as they will be the caretakers of our precious biodiversity in the future
